On its journey to becoming a multidisciplinary and sustainable university, UEH does not stop at academic theories. The smART Hub Living Labs was established as a declaration of transformation. It is not merely a physical space, but an operational ecosystem (a Flywheel) — where technology, art, and urban environmental policy “live” and “breathe” together to realize the vision of a Net Zero Campus.

About smART Hub

smART Hub is a Living Labs ecosystem — an intersection where the boldest ideas in technology, policy, and art are taken off the page to “live” and “breathe” in real-world contexts. It is where four streams — University – Government – Business – Community — converge to collaboratively decode complex urban challenges.

The smART Hub space is positioned as:

  • A platform for implementing “Co-creation for a Net Zero Campus” initiatives.
  • A coordinating hub for Living Labs and urban environmental policy experimentation across the entire UEH system.

smART Hub is the experimental “heart” of the UEH Living Labs network, where a distinctive operational formula is established:
smART Hub = Living Labs + Art + Net Zero Campus.

4 Package Workspace

1. NSUP – “No Single-Use Plastic Journey”
A campaign to shift perceptions around plastic waste through The Zero Pledge commitment, built upon two key pillars: #ReduceForMore and #SortingForChange. The initiative aspires for every individual on campus to become an active link in the journey toward eliminating single-use plastics.

2. Glocal Design (Internationalizing Indigenous Knowledge)
Applying The Glocal Toolkit to standardize local experiences and solutions into internationally adaptable models, addressing sustainable urban challenges through locally rooted yet globally responsive approaches.

3. Towards Net Zero
Developing a Digital Twin of Campus V. Through a real-time data dashboard powered by a sensor network and a sustainable audit system, the project enables intelligent energy and emissions management while providing feedback loops to inform and optimize design solutions.

4. Art for all
Art for Inspiration (The Power of Inspiration)
At smART Hub, art is not merely decorative—it is a transformative tool that awakens emotion, making sustainable lifestyles more beautiful, relatable, and desirable.

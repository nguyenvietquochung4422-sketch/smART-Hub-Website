Our Mission

Where Actions For “Co-Creation For Net Zero Campus” Took Place, The Coordinating Campus For Living Labs And Experiment For Urban Environmental Policy.

Living Lab Procedure

smART Hub is a Living Labs ecosystem — where University, Government, Business, and Community converge to collaboratively decode complex urban challenges.

01.

Preparation

Establishing the foundation through problem clarification, management structuring, and vision identification.

02.

Experimentation

Where ideas meet reality. Resource allocation, 4P platform setup, and solution proposals are tested in real-world contexts.

03.

Evaluation & Replication

Assessing impacts and learning from experience. Proven solutions are scaled up to foster campus-wide transformations.

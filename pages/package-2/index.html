GLOCAL
DESIGN

1. NSUP – “No Single-Use Plastic Journey” A campaign to shift perceptions around plastic waste through The Zero Pledge commitment, built upon two key pillars: #ReduceForMore and #SortingForChange. The initiative aspires for every individual on campus to become an active link in the journey toward eliminating single-use plastics.
2. Glocal Design (Internationalizing Indigenous Knowledge) Applying The Glocal Toolkit to standardize local experiences and solutions into internationally adaptable models, addressing sustainable urban challenges through locally rooted yet globally responsive approaches.
3. Towards Net Zero Developing a Digital Twin of Campus V. Through a real-time data dashboard powered by a sensor network and a sustainable audit system.
